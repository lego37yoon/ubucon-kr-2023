# Health and Safety information

To provide safe environment for every participants, We require every participants to read and follow our Code of Conducts ([See *About* page for details](/en/about)). Waering nametags are also required to enter conference area, participate workshop that required seperate registration as well as social activities such as conference dinner and group tour. For people without nametags, We may ask to leave or deny entrance.
