UbuCon Korea is an event organized by Ubuntu Korea Community. It's for everyone who involved with or interested in Ubuntu, Linux, and other Free and Open source technologies. It is also a place for Ubuntu users and contributors to connect, learn, and share their knowledges each other.

The main theme of UbuCon Korea 2023 is "Ubuntu for Developer Productivity", and it will be hold at Microsoft Korea in Seoul, South Korea.

## Organizing Team

UbuCon Korea 2023 is a not-for-profit conference and a community-driven event made possible by team of volunteers from Ubuntu Korea Community and other FLOSS Communities.
